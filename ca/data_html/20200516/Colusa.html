Public Health

Colusa County Public Health Division

The mission of Colusa County Public Health is to protect and promote the health of all Colusa County residents.


For information and updates regarding COVID-19 visit our COVID-19 page or click here.

California Children’s Services (CCS)

What is California Children’s Services?

A state/county program caring for children with Special medical needs. CCS is a program that helps children with certain diseases, physical limitations or chronic health problems.

Senior Nutrition

Our mission is to assist older adults in Colusa County to live independently by promoting better health, improving nutrition, and reducing social isolation through programs coordinated with nutrition-related supportive services.

Car Seat Check-up

Correctly installing a child safety seat can be challenging. The Maternal Child and Adolescent Health (MCAH) can help. Please call the MCAH program to schedule a time to get your car seat check by a certified child passenger safety technician.

Tobacco Education

The Colusa County Tobacco Education Program works to create smoke-free areas in Colusa County, reduce the impact of tobacco marketing on youth, lower youth access to tobacco and provide educational materials and resources to the public.
