We use the Governor’s Office of Emergency Services report to provide the most accurate information. The Governors report is not updated on the weekends. For this reason, Mariposa County’s Daily Situation Report will not be updated on Saturdays or Sundays.

There is a COVID-19 Testing site sponsored by the State of California Office of Emergency Services and run by OptumServe that is opened as of Tuesday, May 05, 2020 at 7am.

This will not only help individuals who are symptomatic have a smoother pathway for getting tested, but also allow us to routinely test healthcare professionals, first responders, and others who may have regular contact with the public.
 
We highly recommend that team members who have contact with the public get tested once every 14 days. 

GENERAL INFORMATION
 
Site Location: Mariposa Alternative Education Site: 5171 Silva Rd, Mariposa, CA 95338
Testing site hours: Tuesday - Saturday from 7am-7pm.
 
To schedule an appointment please call 1-888-634-1123 or visit https://www.lhi.care/covidtesting
The test is free and you do not need a doctor referral to test. Tests are done by appointment only. You must register for an appointment before arriving to the facility.

This is NOT a serology test.
 
You do not have to be a Mariposa County resident to get tested.
You do not have to be showing symptoms to get tested.
If you need assistance with transportation, please call our COVID-19 Information Line at 209-259-1332

A Message from our County:

On 4/23/2020 Governor Newsom announced that 4/22/2020 was the deadliest day for California with 115 deaths related to COVID-19. This brought the California death toll too 1,469. As the weather is warming up and we are seeing our beautiful spring days we continue to urge you to stay home. Limit your exposure to public places and remember to maintain your 6 feet of social distancing and wash your hands. Remember when you are in public to wear a mask to protect those around you. Apart Today. Community Forever. Mariposa Strong. From our Governor, Stay Safe. Stay Healthy. Stay Connected.
