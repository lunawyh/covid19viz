Mendocino County Public Health is working closely with local health care providers, California Department of Public Health (CDPH) and the Centers for Disease Control & Prevention (CDC) to closely monitor the Coronavirus Disease (COVID-19). This is an emerging, rapidly evolving situation and Mendocino County Public Health will provide updated information as it becomes available.

 

Current COVID-19 Statistics for Mendocino County as of 5/15/2020 at 5 p.m.:

Total Cases: 13

Total Recovered: 12

Total in Isolation: 1

Total Tests: 2299

This is a rapidly changing situation and Public Health will be releasing COVID-19 case numbers and information daily Monday, Wednesday and Friday on this page and on the County's social media accounts.
