Coronavirus



The Lake County Department of Health Services (DHS) continues to work closely with the California Department of Public Health (CDPH) and the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) to monitor the Novel Coronavirus (COVID-19).  

To contact  the Health Department regarding the coronavirus, MHOAC@lakecountyca.gov. 

To contact Public Health for information on Coronavirus call 707-263-8174.

As of April 15, 2020, Lake County Health Services will be discontinuing the daily COVID-19 Dashboards. Information will now be shared on our new “Lake County, California Coronavirus Response Hub.” This hub provides for a centralized location of data allowing for easy access and distribution.  The recently released Story Mapping Data is a part of the Hub. As resource information becomes available, the hub will be updated. Please click on the hub graphic above that will direct you to the site.
