COVID-19 data dashboards

Daily Summary
Dashboard

This data dashboard shows total cases, deaths and demographics. Data are updated daily in the early afternoon and reflect laboratory results reported to the Washington State Department of Health as of midnight the day before.

Long Term Care Facility
Dashboard

This dashboard is updated on a weekly basis and shows the impacts of COVID-19 on residents and staff of long term care facilities.

Race/Ethnicity
Dashboard

This dashboard is updated on a weekly basis and shows the impacts of COVID-19 on communities of color compared to whites in King County, Washington.

Syndromic Surveillance
Dashboard

This dashboard is updated on a weekly basis and shows emergency department visits and hospitalizations for COVID-like illness and pneumonia at King County healthcare facilities.
