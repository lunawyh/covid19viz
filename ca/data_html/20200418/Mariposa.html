Currently there are NO confirmed cases of COVID-19 in Mariposa County. 
Mariposa County Public Health Testing Information as of 04/17/2020 at 9:00 am.

Total Tested:     89
Total Pending:    5
Total Negative:  84
Total Positive:     0
Total Deaths:       0

These numbers do not include additional testing through the commercial lab system, hospitals and clinics. Although Mariposa County Public Health does not receive information on the number of tests sent through those systems, Public Health is notified of all positive result tests.
